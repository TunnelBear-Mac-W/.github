## Overview

TunnelBear Advanced is a user-friendly VPN service distinguished by its charming bear-themed interface, commitment to transparency through annual independent security audits, and simple one-click protection that makes VPN use accessible to non-technical users. It proves that strong security and approachable design are not mutually exclusive, providing genuine protection in a package that anyone can understand and use.

One-click connection activates VPN protection with a single click, connecting to the fastest available server without requiring country selection, protocol configuration, or any technical knowledge. The simplicity is deliberate — the application makes the secure choice the easy choice.

Annual security audits by independent firms review the complete VPN infrastructure, client applications, and privacy practices, publishing the results publicly. The audit commitment provides verifiable assurance that the service operates as advertised, addressing the trust problem that the VPN industry faces.

GhostBear mode disguises VPN traffic as regular HTTPS traffic, enabling VPN use in restrictive network environments that block standard VPN protocols. The obfuscation handles the increasingly common scenario of networks that actively interfere with VPN connections.

For users who want VPN protection that is genuinely easy to use with independently verified security, TunnelBear Advanced is the VPN that makes online privacy accessible and trustworthy.
